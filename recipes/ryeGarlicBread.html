Garlic Rye Bread Recipe

Back to Recipes This is the best fucking thing I've ever eaten I'm so hungry right now and I can't stop thinking about how badly I want to eat this with some beer right now take me back.

Ingredients

  • Rye Bread
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Garlic Sauce

Steps

  1. Prep pan by melting butter
  2. Pan-sear rye bread in butter
  3. While bread is starting to become crispy, coat with garlic
  4. Make garlic sauce and enjoy!
