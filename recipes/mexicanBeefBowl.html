Mexican Beef Bowl Recipe

Back to Recipes

This is a bowl of some mexican bowl which is a meal prep that uses beef, in case you get bored of eating chicken every week.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef 90/10
  • Rice
  • Salsa
  • Mixed Veggies

Steps

  1. Cook rice and veggies
  2. Coat beef in taco seasoning,pan fry
  3. Mix rice with beef, add lettuce, salsa and cheese
